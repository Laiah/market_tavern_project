ABOUT US

Opened in the start of 2015, The Market Tavern Bordeaux GastroPub is on the forefront of modern pub food and tasty craft beers on tap. Inspired by the modern gastro-pubs of London, TMT is a small family oriented establishment in the heart of Bordeaux’s chic and historic Chartrons district.
Welcoming you with open arms seven days a week from 11am until 2am, this family owned pub has 40 seats inside and 40 seats on a terrace with table service all day. During lunchtimes you can enjoy the “Formule du Midi” that includes a selection of different starters, main courses and desserts that changes every day using fresh produce, and a set evening Menu with our pub classics such as Beer Battered Fish and Chips and a homemade Tavern Pie.
What better to accompany your meal than a fresh, tasty beer? One of the first pubs in Bordeaux to collaborate with British brewery Greene King, The Market Tavern has 10 different beers on tap ranging from British Ales to Craft Lager and IPAs.
The Market Tavern Bordeaux GastroPub has an international and bilingual staff (The Marketeers) that are always ready and willing to help with any questions customers may have about the beers, the food or the city of Bordeaux.

THE BORDEAUX PUB GROUP

We are a group of three family owned and operated English pubs in Bordeaux: The Houses of Parliament, The HMS Victory and The Market Tavern. We arrived in Bordeaux 15 years ago and have worked hard to offer you three very individual English pubs, all with the same values: good drinks, good service, good fun!

The Houses of Parliament

One of the most well known and long established English Pubs in Bordeaux, The Houses of Parliament is an unmissable watering-hole for locals of all ages, students and tourists alike. The ‘HoP’, as the Bordelais call it, is situated in the heart of the picturesque Saint Pierre area, the bustling historic centre of Bordeaux that boasts an incredible range of restaurants and bars.
This unique pub is open 7 days a week until 2am and Louis, Alex and their friendly and hard working gang strive to offer you something different every night; a wide range of beers and cocktails, great Happy Hour specials, Live Music, Sport on the big screen and our infamous Sunday Pub Quiz!
If you’re looking for a great crowd of supporters or a great after party, The Houses is the place to be in Bordeaux centre!

The HMS Victory

The HMS Victory is the largest English Pub in Bordeaux, spread over two floors and a 100 seater terrace, situated at the Place de la Victoire end of Bordeaux’s famous pedestrian shopping street, Rue Sainte Catherine. With its two bars, two big screens and 6 HD TVs it has quickly become a sporting headquarters for diehard fans and players alike.
Run by former pro rugby player Lugha Verling and his multinational crew, whichever team you support you’ll always find a warm welcome and amazing atmosphere. If you’re looking to party in Bordeaux, the weekend starts on Wednesday with our beer pong tournament, followed by the town’s Student Night on Thursday. The weekend’s live music and funky DJ sets will guarantee you a good time!
If you’re looking to party in Bordeaux, the HMS is a guaranteed good time! Be sure and come over any evening, and especially on the weekends, for live matches and live music!

The Market Tavern

Situated in the chic and charming Quartier des Chartrons, The Market Tavern is Bordeaux’s newest English Gastropub, serving fresh creations from their kitchen and fine Greene King ales from the bar. Are you looking to get stuck into a great burger while watching a game, recover from the night before with an English breakfast or prepare for the evening ahead with a delicious aperitif on a sunny terrace? Andrew, Mike and their team are waiting to greet you!
If you’re looking for a place to eat while watching a match or drinking a few pints, be sure and reserve a table so that we can give you the VIP treatment!
